Iterating Over Designs

Added "active" state - allowing new (and old) users to reselect buttons, in case of mistake

Releasing a click off of any buttons - keeps the last selected button, more intuitive than always going back to a "default" state

Removing "focused" state for keyboard users - who do not need the same distinction between highlighted vs selected buttons as mouse users do (when hovering)

Learnability vs efficiency tradeoff - undoability of pressing enter. If the enter key is pressed, ultimately one of the selected buttons WILL get pressed. Tab can be used to toggle selection when enter key is being held down, which users may not be familiar with. However, I made this choice as a tradeoff with efficiency - every-day users may not want to have to click a button twice to to actually invoke the action of pressing the button.
